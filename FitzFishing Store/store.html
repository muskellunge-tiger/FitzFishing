Welcome to the store. On this page you will be able to purchase jig heads with extended shanks on them. You will be able to select the weight, color, size, and quantity of the jig heads. Please remember that the weight and size selections are two different things. The weight selection is how heavy the jig head will be. While the size selection is based on how large of a tube jig you plan on putting the jig head in. The reason we need to know the size of tube jig need is because we need to know how long the shank needs to be. There are a few reasons why not only using tube jigs is a great option while fishing, but it is sometimes important to have an extended shank on them that allows the hook to go farther down the jigs skirt. This will allow smaller and sometimes larger more tentative fish to have a better chance of hooking up.
