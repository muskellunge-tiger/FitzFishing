Introduction

Hi, my name is Matt Fitzgerald and welcome to the FitzFishing Store. On this site we are offering tube jig heads with extended shanks on them. There are a few reasons why having an extended shank on your jig head is a good idea, these reasons are explained in detail on the store page. To go along with the store there is also a "Tips and Tricks" page. This page will give you some of my favorite tube jig fishing methods, so that you can have better success on your next fishing outing. Finally, there is a "Species Effectiveness" page. This page shows, based on my own experience and opinion, how effective tube jigs are when fishing for certain species. Please note that there will not be every single species of fish under the sun on that page because there are over 28,000 species of fish on this planet. I have just chosen some popular freshwater fish that people usually fish for.

My Fishing Experience

I am a fourth-generation fisherman. I have been fishing avidly for 10 of my 19 years of life. I heavily fish Northern Utah lakes and ponds. My main area of expertise in fishing is Trout fishing and more specifically Trout fishing with tube jigs. I pretty much learned how to fish for Trout using tube jigs. During those 10 years I have gone on hundreds of trips and have caught over 1,000 fish and have probably seen close to 1,000 others caught. During all of those trips I have gathered tons of information about fish behavior and some feeding habits. Because a very large portion of those fish have been caught using tube jigs, I have multiple methods that you can find on the "Tips and Tricks" page, so that the next time you fish with tube jigs you will be able to catch a lot more fish.
