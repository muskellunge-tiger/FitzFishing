Kaysville Past Reports

The ponds are full, ice free, and the fishing is fast right now. The best lure to use is a small yellow Atomic tube jig using a slow retrieve method. This lure works so well that it doesn't require being tipped with bait most of the time to catch fish. However, if you aren't getting any bites then tipping you jig with pink maggots is a good idea. Another method that worked if the tube jig wasn't working was bottom fishing half a worm and half a marshmallow on about a 6" leader. (Report from: 2/23/20)
