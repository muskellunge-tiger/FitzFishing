Kaysville Past Reports

Even though the ponds where recently stocked the fishing is absolutely horrible. Almost every method under the sun does not work. We saw multiple fisherman catch nothing, and many said they didn't even get any bits. (Report from: 4/5/19)

Fishing has really slowed over the past few days. The possible reason for this might be due to the crowds from last weekend catching, and taking a lot of the fish out of the pond. Fishing a green tube jig with pink maggots still work occasionally, and there is still a decent amount of 18-22" Rainbow Trout in the pond, but there is not near as many as there where the week before. I suggest that you wait for the pond to be re-stocked if you want to get on a good bite. (Report from: 3/19/19)
