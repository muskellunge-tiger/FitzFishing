Strategies

Fishing Hobbs in 2018 was pretty slow, but was definatily better in the spring if you wanted to catch trout. While spinners like silver, green, and red Jakes where a great option wolly bugger flies, and just flies in general were the best. One of the methods we used with flies was just flat lining them this worked cause the trout would sometimes swim very close to short allowing someone with a spinning reel to cast to the trout, and watch them attack the fly. Another method was putting a fly on a bobber, and waiting for the fly to get hit by a trout. It was this method that we where using when a trout hit the fly in a specacular way. Which really showed how aggressively they would hit flies. You can watch this happen in the video below.

Unfortunatilly due to the very hot 2018 summer the lake level fell a long ways, and because of this most of trout population was killed off. Thankfully they where able to reproduce before there death, so there is now a ton of very small uncatchable trout in the lake, but I think the lake will make a recovery in 2019 as long as there is a good winter. Please know that if you are the age of 14 or older you are required the have an Access Permit, which is free, along with your valid fishing license.
