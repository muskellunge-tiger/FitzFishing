Spring

The one shore fishing trip we made to Strawberry in the spring was right after ice off. While the fishing was a little slow there was quite a few methods that produced some nice Rainbows and Cutthroats. The main thing that you would want to look for was steep drop-offs near shore. The easiest way to do this is to look for where the shore line is steep or on top of or around cliffs. The first method we used was casting 2" white tube jigs with a 3/8oz. head. The retrieve that we used was a jig and reel down method. For an in-depth tutorial on how to use this retrieve please read the casting section on tube jigs below. While this method was not the best numbers producer it did produce the biggest fish of the day, a 20" Cutthroat.
