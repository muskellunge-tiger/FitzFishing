Strategies

Due to virus restrictions in April, we were unable to fish Rockport in the early part of the year like we normally do, and we were only able to go once this year and that one time we went the fishing was pretty slow. We were never able to find something that worked consistently. The method that somewhat worked the best for us was trolling pop gear and worm. The best pop gear was 4-6 bladed silver and gold willow leaf with half a worm 6-18" behind the pop gear. We usually trolled around 1mph. We also tried spinners and spoons, and only caught a couple fish on a silver spoon. We also tried tube jigs, and while we got bites, we were unable to catch any fish on them.
