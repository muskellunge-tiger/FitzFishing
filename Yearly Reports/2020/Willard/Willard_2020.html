Strategies

The one time we went to Willard this year the fishing was extremely slow. The only fish we caught was a 12" Crappie while trolling for Wipers. We caught the Crappie on a 2" silver Ratl-trap while trolling around 2mph. We also had a follow on a white deep diving crankbait. But all in all, the fishing was not good the day we went.
