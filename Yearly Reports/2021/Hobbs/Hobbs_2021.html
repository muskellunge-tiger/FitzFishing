Strategies

Overview

The few times we went to Hobbs this year the fishing was pretty good, by Hobbs standards. Spinners and spoons were the way to go for us when fishing for Trout. Most of the time the fish were not very far from shore because that is where most of the small bait fish were, so making shoreline casts with spinners and spoons was a great option. The water clarity was also good all the times we went which made the fishing better with spoons as well because the fish could easily see our lures.

Best Lures for Trout

  • Red, White, and Gold Junior Kamlooper
  • Li'l Green Jake
  • The best retrieve to use with the spinners and spoons was usually just a consistant speed that was just fast enough to get the fish to react.

Best Lures for Bluegill

  • Small Pink Trout Magnet
  • Small Flies
