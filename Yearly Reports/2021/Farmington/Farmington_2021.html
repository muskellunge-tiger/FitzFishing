Strategies

Overview

The one time that we went to Farmington this year, the fishing was very slow. There was a lot of fish activity on the surface, but they did not seem to be wanting to hit any moving baits, and it was hard to use any spinners or spoons well because there was a lot of grass in the pond and because of the low water level the lures would get caught in the grass sometimes. While we were able to catch a few fish on spinners and spoons, we later realized that fishing on the bottom or with flies was the better option because the fish were not as active as they seemed.

Best Spinners and Spoons

  • Kamloopers
  • Pot-O-Gold's
  • Li'l Jakes

Best Retrieve

  • Fast enough to keep it out of the grass.
