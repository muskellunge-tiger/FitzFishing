Strategies

Overview

For the couple times that we went to East Canyon this year, the fishing was very good. The fish were very active pretty much the enitre day most of the time and there was many different ways and lure that you could use to catch the fish.

Best Strategies

  • Best Spoons and Spinners
    • Red, White, and Gold Junior Kamloopers
    • Silver Jakes
  • Best Jigging and Drift Trolling Lures
    • 1" White Beetle Jigs
    • 1" Pink or White Trout Magnets
  • Best Trolling Set Ups
    • 4 Bladed Silver or Gold Willow Leaf Pop Gear
    • 5 Bladed Colorado Multi Color Pop Grear
    • Silver Dodgers

Jigging and Drift Trolling Tips

  • All our jigs were tipped with either corn, pink maggots, or a small piece of worm.
  • When jigging, not moving your jig at all (dead-sticking) was the best option for when the fish came up to look at it.
  • If you ever got a bite, set the hook the hook and then the line went slack right away. This usually didn't mean you lost the fish, it usually meant that the fish headed straight for the surface and was going to jump right away.

Trolling Tips

  • Best trolling speed was 1 to 1.5MPH.
  • All our pop gear was trailed with either a worm or pink or green squids.
  • We usually would tip our squids with pink maggots to add flavor and scent.
