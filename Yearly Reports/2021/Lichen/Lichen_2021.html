Strategies

Overview

The one canoe trip out on Lichen Lake that we made was a pretty successfull trip. We were able to catch a Muskie and my buddy caught a couple tiny Smallmouth Bass in the hour or two that we were out there in the morning.

Best Lures

  • Jumbo Gold Pot-O-Gold
  • Large Fire Tiger Spoon

Tips

  • In our short time out there, we found that fishing along shore that had rocks, grass, or both was a great way to get some hits from the tiny Smallmouth that were up in the cover.
  • The place we caught the Muskie from was off the wind blown side island in the middle of the lake off a point that had a large amount of grass coming off from the island and point.
