Welcome to the Current Reports page. This page will provide fishing reports from recent trips that we have made to lakes and ponds. There will be up to 4 reports provided on this page. If you would like to see past reports for individual lakes and ponds please go to the Past Reports page. A report will be removed from this page regardless of the circumstances after 3 weeks, and reports from the past reports page will be removed after 4 weeks. After a lake or pond reaches more than 3 reports the oldest report will be removed regardless of the time it has been up. There will be no duplicated lakes on the Current Reports part, but you find the others in the Past Reports. Please know that the rate in which new reports are entered may very depending on the time of year.

Reports

Lost Creek

Fishing is starting to slow down from shore. It seems the only way to catch any fish is to use Kamlooper spoons with the best colors being red and gold or yellow and gold. Make sure to retrieve slowly. Most of the fish are very small Rainbows, but there is the occasional Cutthroat, Tiger, and bigger Rainbows. (Report from: 5/25/19)

Strawberry

The lake is ice free, and the water is fairly clear. Fishing from shore is slowing down, but you are still able to catch nice Rainbows and Cutts. Try to steep drop offs near shore, lake the dam on the Soldier Creek side. Fishing white tub jigs with a 3/8oz. head tipped with chub meat will get a few bites. Large silver or gold spoons like Kamloopers and Pot-o-Golds will catch good sized Rainbows and Cutts at a pretty good rate. If you see small chub swimming near the shore that are being attacked by Cutthroats, use large Lucky Craft minnowbaits. Bait fishing for us was not working at all. (Report from: 5/13/19)
