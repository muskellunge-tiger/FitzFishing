Reports

Good Ski Lake

Fishing is starting to pick up even more now that the pond has been unfrozen for a couple more days. There are 2 really good methods to use they are: bottom fishing and fishing with 1.5-2” tube jigs or 1” Marabou's. When bottom fishing the best bait to use is half a worm and half a marshmallow on about a 6" leader. When fishing tube jigs the best colors to use are yellow and orange, but other colors like green and white will also likely work. When using Marabou’s, the best color is green and white. We recommend that you tip you jigs with some kind of bait, we used pink maggots, to increase bites. When fishing the jigs, it is best to either bottom bounce or cast them out about 15 to 20 feet and just let it sit on the bottom. If you are in an area that has a lot of fish then this method will sometimes get you bites the second the jig hits the bottom. If that doesn't happen then within about a minute you will get bit. If your jig is sitting for more than a couple of minutes without a bite then recast it in a different area or try bottom bouncing to cover more area till you find them. (Report from: 2/26/20)

Kaysville

The ponds are full, ice free, and the fishing is fast right now. The best lure to use is a small yellow Atomic tube jig using a slow retrieve method. This lure works so well that it doesn't require being tipped with bait most of the time to catch fish. However, if you aren't getting any bites then tipping you jig with pink maggots is a good idea. Another method that worked if the tube jig wasn't working was bottom fishing half a worm and half a marshmallow on about a 6" leader. (Report from: 2/23/20)

Strawberry

Fishing is fair right now. What we found was that slightly deeper depths was the way to go. The depth that we fished at was around 55 feet. While there was a lot of fish coming through on the bottom, it was hard at times to get them to bite. However, if fish came through higher in the water column then it would be a lot easier to get them to bite. The be lures to use are 2 to 3" white or pearl tube jigs tipped with Chub. We also added a "stinger hook" because the fish tend to bite the tails. Marabou's also work well especially for fish that come through at around 15 feet. (Report from: 2/16/20)
