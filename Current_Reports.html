Welcome to the Current Reports page. This page will provide fishing reports from recent trips that we have made to lakes and ponds. There will be up to 4 reports provided on this page. If you would like to see past reports for individual lakes and ponds please go to the Past Reports page. A report will be removed from this page regardless of the circumstances after 3 weeks, and reports from the past reports page will be removed after 4 weeks. After a lake or pond reaches more than 3 reports the oldest report will be removed regardless of the time it has been up. There will be no duplicated lakes on the Current Reports part, but you find the others in the Past Reports. Please know that the rate in which new reports are entered may very depending on the time of year.

Reports

Strawberry

The slush on the lake is very bad. Most of the fishing are around 20 feet below the ice with some running a couple of feet below the ice and some near the bottom. Smaller tube jigs are the best option. The best colors are white and green. Tipping your jigs with worms or Chub meat will up your bite rate. THE ROAD TO THE MARINA WILL CLOSE FOR THE SEASON ON MONDAY (MARCH 18) DUE TO HIGH SNOW LEVELS. (Report from: 3/14/19)

Kaysville

With the warmer temperatures the fish are active on the surface, and eating small tube jigs with the best color being bright green. Fly fishing also works well. Most of the fish are 10-12", but there are plenty of 18-20" trout and some might be even bigger. Tipping your jigs with small pieces of worms or pink maggots will up your bite rate. Fishing on the bottom won't really catch you anything. (Report from: 3/12/19)
