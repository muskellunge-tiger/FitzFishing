Strawberry

Jensen

When we went to Jensen the lake was mostly covered with very thin ice, so there was not a lot of fishing spots. But because the ice was so thin, I'm sure it will all melt in a day or two. The method that worked best was fishing rainobw powerbait on the bottom with about a 2-foot leader. This caught us 2 fish, and got many more bites. Another method that worked was casting small 1 inch tube jigs, and slowly reeling them back. This caught us 1 fish, and got a few more bites. All in all, fishing was very slow, but I think once the ice goes away and you are able to fish more of the lake the fishing will pick up. (Report from: 2/8/19)

Posted by: Matt Fitzgerald
