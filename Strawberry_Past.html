Strawberry Past Reports

The lake is ice free, and the water is fairly clear. Fishing from shore is slowing down, but you are still able to catch nice Rainbows and Cutts. Try to steep drop offs near shore, lake the dam on the Soldier Creek side. Fishing white tub jigs with a 3/8oz. head tipped with chub meat will get a few bites. Large silver or gold spoons like Kamloopers and Pot-o-Golds will catch good sized Rainbows and Cutts at pretty good rate. If you see small chub swimming near the shore that are being attacked by Cutthroats, use large Lucky Craft minnowbaits. Bait fishing for us was not working at all. (Report from: 5/13/19)
