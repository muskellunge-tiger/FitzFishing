Strawberry Past Reports

Water temperature is in the low 60s. Fishing has slowed a little when casting white tube jigs, but has gotten much faster jigging them. We recommend using 2" jigs tipped with Chub meat. Casting red and gold, and yellow and gold Kamloopers got a lot of bites. You may have to let the Kamlooper sink a little, but most of the time fish will be on the surface. When trolling for Kokanee, we found that using silver willow leaf and tomahawk pop gear with wedding rings and squids tipped with pink maggots would do pretty good. Just remember to try different depths till you find where the fish are. (Report from: 7/2/19)
