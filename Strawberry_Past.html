Strawberry Past Reports

The lake is fully frozen, and the ice is thick enough to walk on safely. Fishing is pretty slow. Most larger jigs don't work, it is the smaller ones that do the best. The best one for us was a 1" yellow tube jig tipped with one or two pink maggots. A small white minnow shaped lure tipped with a wax worm worked good high in the water column. Another jig that worked was a white and green tube jig tipped with Chub meat. The fish are in pretty much the whole water column, but the fish that come through high, 15 feet or shallower, will hit the best. Most of the deeper fish, around 30 feet, will look for a long time but never really bite. Most of the fish we caught were small, around 14", but there is some giant slot-buster sized fish mixed in with the smaller ones. (Report from: 12/29/19)
