Strawberry Past Reports

Water temperature is around 65 to 66 degrees in the morning, and 68 to 70 degrees in the afternoon. Most of the fish are 35 to 50 feet down in about 60 feet of water. When it is calm casting and jigging white tube jigs tipped with Chub meat works well. When the wind picks up jigging the tube jigs while drifting with the wind is also a good method. If the jigs aren't working, then trolling silver or silver and gold willow leaf pop gear with a worm will catch a few fish. You will have to use weight, or down riggers in order to get your gear down to where the fish are. (Report from: 8/13/19)
