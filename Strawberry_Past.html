Strawberry Past Reports

The slush on the lake is very bad. Most of the fishing are around 20 feet below the ice with some running a couple of feet below the ice and some near the bottom. Smaller tube jigs are the best option. The best colors are white and green. Tipping your jigs with worms or Chub meat will up your bite rate. THE ROAD TO THE MARINA WILL CLOSE FOR THE SEASON ON MONDAY (MARCH 18) DUE TO HIGH SNOW LEVELS. (Report from: 3/14/19)
