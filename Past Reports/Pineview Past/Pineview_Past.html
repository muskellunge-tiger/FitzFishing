Pineview Past Reports

The ice is around 6 to 7" thick. However, the narrows is still mostly open water, so that area is not accessible right now. The fishing is fairly slow for panfish. The best lure for use was a 1" green tube jig tipped with a piece of Perch meat. The only other jig that worked for us was a 1" yellow Atomic tube. Pretty much all the fish are on the bottom in around 30 feet of water. (Report From: 12/30/20)
