Holmes Past Reports

Fishing is still pretty good with spoons. Most of the fish are still pretty far offshore so using larger spoons remains the way to go. The best spoon right now seems to be a fire tiger Daredevl, and a junior red, white, and gold Kamlooper will also still work but not as well because it can't cast as far out as the Daredevl. (Report From: 4/28/21)

Fishing is still good with spoons. However, because the water is starting to warm up, the fish are starting to move farther offshore into deeper water. This means that you have to use bigger spoons to get out farther to them. The best spoon right now, is a fire tiger Daredevl. Sometimes a junior red, white, and gold Kamlooper will work as well, but it doesn't get out as far as the Daredevl so it sometimes struggles to get bites. (Report From: 4/20/21)

The water temperature has increased and because of that the fishing is hot. Spoons are the best way to go because most of the fish are still a ways offshore, so having a little extra casting distance is key. The spoon that dominated everything else that we used was a red, white, and gold junior Kamlooper. You may have to try varying your retrieve speed to get them to bite. Sometimes they like it moving faster and sometimes they like it moving slower. The fish are also heavily schooled up right now, so if you catch a fish in one area, keep casting in that same area until the school moves on. Then try casting in slightly different areas to try to find that same school or a different school. Just always keep in mind that the schools are constantly moving around. (Report From: 4/9/21)
