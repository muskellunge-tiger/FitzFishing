Farmington Past Reports

Water clarity is excellent and the fishing is red hot. Spoons are the best lures to use. The best spoons are red and white or orange and silver junior Kamloopers and copper Pot-O-Golds. Number 2 silver and green Bluefox's and lil silver Jakes was also work, but not near as good as the Kamloopers and Pot-O-Golds. While the fishing maybe extremely good right now, with a cold front coming in it could cause the fishing to slow because of how cold it will get. (Report from: 10/21/20)
