Strawberry Past Reports

Water temperature is around 70 degrees the entire day now. However, the fishing is still red hot. 2.5-3" white tube jigs are still the way to. You also don't have to tip your jigs with anything, but we still recommend tipping your jigs with Chub meat. Most of the fish are suspended in about 35 to 45' in 55 to 60' feet of water.
