Strawberry Past Reports

Fishing is very good right now. The water temperature is mainly in the low 60's. The best method is casting or jigging a 2" white tube jig tipped with Chub meat. Spoons will also sometimes work as well. The best spoons to use are yellow and gold or orange and gold Kamloopers. The best depths to target are 40 to 60 feet. (Report from: 7/3/20)

Fishing is really improving. The best lure to use is still a 2" white tube jig with a 3/8oz. head tipped with Chub or Sucker meat. Other lures that work are small Lucky Crafts and spoons or spinners. Trolling pop gear and worm can be a great way to catch a good amount of fish as well if the artificial bite is not working. Fishing is fairly slow right now. The water tempurature is around 55 to 58 degrees depending on the time of day. Most of the fish are 25 to 40 feet down in 40 to 60 feet of water. The best lure to use is a 2" white tube jig with a 3/8oz. head tipped with Chub or Sucker meat. The best way to catch the fish with the tube jig is to cast or jig. Trolling pop gear or dodger with a a worm or small pink squid. Spinners and spoons didn't work for us even though there was a fair amount of fish breaking the surface. (Report from: 6/5/20)
