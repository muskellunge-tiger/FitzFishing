Strawberry Past Reports

Fishing is fairly slow right now. The water tempurature is around 55 to 58 degrees depending on the time of day. Most of the fish are 25 to 40 feet down in 40 to 60 feet of water. The best lure to use is a 2" white tube jig with a 3/8oz. head tipped with Chub or Sucker meat. The best way to catch the fish with the tube jig is to cast or jig. Trolling pop gear or dodger with a a worm or small pink squid. Spinners and spoons didn't work for us even though there was a fair amount of fish breaking the surface. (Report from: 6/6/20)
