Lost Creek Past Reports

Fishing is slow from shore right now. We did catch a couple of fish by using a 2.5" white tube jig tipped with Chub meat, and one fish on a bobber with half a worm around 1 to 2 feet below the bobber. We also tried fishing on the bottom with half a worm and half a marshmallow with no luck. (Report from: 11/17/20)
