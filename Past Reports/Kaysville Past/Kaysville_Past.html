Kaysville Past Reports

Fishing is fair to slow right now. The fish seem to be feeding on bottom and on top. So, bottom fishing and tube jig fishing is the best way to go. For the bottom, half or a thrid of a worm and half a marshmallow on a 6 to 18" leader works best. For the jigs, small green or pink jigs tipped with a pink maggot work best. We recommend fishing on sunny and calm days especially if you plan on fishing with the jigs. strong>(Report From: 3/8/21)
