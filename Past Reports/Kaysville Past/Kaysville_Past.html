Kaysville Past Reports

Fishing is pretty slow right now. There is almost no method that works consistently. We tried fishing multiple different jigs, on the bottom, and bobber and a worm. We only caught a few fish on a bobber and a worm, and couple fish on a few different jigs. Those being a small green tube jig, a small yellow Atomic tube, and a small white beetle jig with a black jig head. It seems that if there are clouds blocking the sun, it is extremely hard to almost impossible to catch any fish. (Report From: 3/14/21)
