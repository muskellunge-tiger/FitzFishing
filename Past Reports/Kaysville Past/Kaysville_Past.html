Kaysville Past Reports

Fishing is pretty slow right now. The two methods that worked well for us were bottom fishing worm and a marshmallow and slowly retrieving or bottom bouncing small green tube jigs tipped with a pink maggot. The tube jig seemed to work best early and then the bottom worked best later along with bottom bouncing the jig. (Report From: 3/7/22)
