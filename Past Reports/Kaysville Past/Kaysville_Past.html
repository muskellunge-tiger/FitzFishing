Kaysville Past Reports

Fishing is good for Catfish and Wiper right now. The best thing to do is fish on the bottom with 1/4 to 1/2 worm and half a marshmallow on about a 12 to 18" leader. Most of the Catfish are very small and the occasional Wipers are also small. (Report From: 6/8/21)
