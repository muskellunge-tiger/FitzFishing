Strategies

When fishing Willard we didn't have much success, but we did find some lures that got a decent amount of hits. We found that Perch colored lures got a good number of bits, but 3" white crankbaits with large lips where the best to use. We also heard that fishing in the evening can be a great time to catch big Wipers. We tried this, and unfortunately didn't catch any Wipers, but we did catch a Smallmouth Bass.
