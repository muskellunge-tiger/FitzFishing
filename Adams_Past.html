Adams Past Reports

Water clarity is great and there is a lot of fish active on the surface near shore. Fishing with spoons is pretty much the only way to catch these fish. The best spoons to use are: red and white junior Kamloopers, fire tiger or rainbow patterned Daredevils, and gold and red Pot-O-Golds. If you fish with bait on top or bottom you will not catch anything. The same goes for fly fishing. (Report from: 10/8/19)

Due to a recnet stocking the fishing has picked up. Fishing Kamloopers or junior Kamloopers is the way to go. The best colors to use are red, gold, and white or yellow and gold. Orange and gold my also work, but due to the fact that the water is no super clear it doesn't do as good as the other colors. (Report from: 9/26/19)

Fishing is very slow right now mostly likely due to the warm temperatures. We think in around a week or two the fishing will pick-up. (Report from: 9/12/19)
