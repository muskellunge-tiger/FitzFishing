Strategies

When ice fishing for Perch we found that small jigs worked well. On a good day you can catch over 100 Perch, but we were unable to get anywhere near that. Mainly because it was the first time we had ice fished for Perch which we found that it we very hard to hook these fish because they are so small. We also tried to catch Tiger Muskie through the ice and on the boat, but we were unable to hook up on one.
