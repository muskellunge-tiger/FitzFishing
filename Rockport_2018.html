Strategies

Fishing at Rockport in 2018 never seemed to be good at all. Sometimes it seemed like there was not even any life in the lake which is very unusual for Rockport. The one day we caught fish from shore the best lure to use was a 2" white twisty tailed storm minnow, and the one fish we caught through the ice was on a small jig. Beyond that we never could really get anything going.
