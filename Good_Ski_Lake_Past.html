Good Ski Lake Past Reports

Fishing is great with tube jigs. You will want to use 1.5" orange, yellow, or white tube jigs tipped with a pink maggot. You also want to use a lighter jig head with a longer hook, so it sinks slower and so you have a better hookup chance. The best method to catch them is using a bottom bouncing method. Most of the fish are around 12", but there is plenty of 13-15 inchers as well. (Report from: 11/15/19)

Fishing is pretty good. There is a lot of fish on the surface, so fishing spoons and spinners works very well. The spoon we found to work the best was a fire tiger Daredevil, and the best spinner was a green Jake. While spoons and spinners will catch you a lot of fish, fishing a tube jig tipped with a pink maggot will get you a ton of bites. The best tube jig to use is a 1.5" orange tube jig with a lighter jig head inside. You will want to fish this using a bottom bouncing method, or a slow retrieve method. Most of the fish are in the 12" range. (Report from: 11/6/19)
