Strategies

When fishing Holmes spinners and spoons seemed to be the best option to catch trout. Spoons like Cast Masters and Kamloopers worked a lot better then spinners like Jakes and Bluefoxes. The method that worked best with the spoons was casting them as far as you could, or where you saw fish jumping and reeling it back at a decent speed. Unfortunately, due to the very hot summer a lot of the planter sized trout died due to major water level changes, but it looked like they where able to reproduce before they died. So, with a good winter the pond should go back to normal soon. Please know that if you are the age of 14 or older you are required the have an Access Permit, which is free, along with your valid fishing license.
