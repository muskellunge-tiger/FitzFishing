Welcome to the Current Reports page. This page will provide fishing reports from recent trips that we have made to lakes and ponds. There will be up to 4 reports provided on this page. If you would like to see past reports for individual lakes and ponds please go to the Past Reports page. A report will be removed from this page regardless of the circumstances after 3 weeks, and reports from the past reports page will be removed after 1 month. After a lake or pond in the past reports reaches more than 3 reports the oldest report will be removed regardless of the time it has been up. There will be no duplicated lakes on the Current Reports part, but you can find the others in the Past Reports. Please know that the rate in which new reports are entered may very depending on the time of year.

Reports

Good Ski

Fishing is still fairly slow right now, but the lake is full and the water is cool. The best method for us was fishing on the bottom with worm and a marshmallow. You will have to cast a little farther because most of the fish are still out in the deeper water, but we hope they will be moving in shallower in the coming weeks. We tried spinners, spoons, and tube jigs as well, but with no luck. (Report from: 9/2/20)

Pineview

The Crappie fishing is very fast right now. There are tons of large schools of Crappie around the lake. The best jig for us was a 1" yellow atomic tube jig tipped with either Perch meat or a pink maggot. When trying to find these schools, it seemed that around 20 feet was the best depth. As for the Muskie fishing, it is extremely slow. Due to the amount and size of the Crappie schools we believe that a good portion of the Muskie have died because we did see multiple dead Muskie along shorelines. (Report from: 8/27/20)
