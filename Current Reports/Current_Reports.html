Welcome to the Current Reports page. This page will provide fishing reports from recent trips that we have made to lakes and ponds. There will be up to 4 reports provided on this page. If you would like to see past reports for individual lakes and ponds please go to the Past Reports page. A report will be removed from this page regardless of the circumstances after 3 weeks, and reports from the past reports page will be removed after 1 month. After a lake or pond in the past reports reaches more than 3 reports the oldest report will be removed regardless of the time it has been up. There will be no duplicated lakes on the Current Reports part, but you can find the others in the Past Reports. Please know that the rate in which new reports are entered may very depending on the time of year.

Reports

Lost Creek

Fishing is beginning to slow down a little probably due to the fact that it is getting late in the season. The best method right now is casting a 2.5" white tube jig tipped with Chub meat. The other method that works is fishing on the bottom with worm and a marshmallow on a 1 to 3' leader. Most of the fish are close to the shore, so you do not have to cast out very far at all to catch the fish. (Report From: 11/23/21)

Good Ski Lake

The water clarity is very good right now, and there seems to be a lot of fish activity, but the fishing isn't that great right now. Bottom fishing a worm and a marshmallow was what worked best for us. Bottom bouncing a tube jig got us a few hits and 1 one fish and spinners did nothing. So, overall, the fishing is not that hot at the moment. (Report From: 11/12/21)

Holmes

The water level has come up some and the clarity has dropped too. However, fishing is still okay. Most of the fish are on or near the bottom now most of the time, so bottom fishing with bait or bottom bouncing jigs are what will work best. For bottom fishing, worm and a marshmallow on about a 1 to 1.5' leader is the best, and for jigs, a small white beetle jig tipped with a pink maggot is the way to go. The fish are not very far out right now, so you don't need to cast bottom gear out very far to have success. (Report From: 11/3/21)
