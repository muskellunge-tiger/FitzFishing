Jensen Past Reports

There is a good amount of fish in the pond due to recent stocking. Fish are mainly hitting on the bottom. Try using worm and a marshmallow or corn and a marshmallow. Occasionally a fish will hit a spinner on the surface, but it is very unlikely. The Pelicans and ducks are also a problem because they cause some much chaos on the surface that it scares the fish off. (Report from: 3/25/19)
