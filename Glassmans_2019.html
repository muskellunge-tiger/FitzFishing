Strategies

The one time we fished Glassmans it was pretty good. The water clarity was great and the fish were active. Fishing with small spinners like junior Kamloopers, small silver jakes, and small rooster tails worked very well. While the fishing was great and all, the lack of space at the pond is really what killed it. By the time you get more than five people fishing it gets really cramped. This is due to the fact that most of the lake is covered with lily pads, so there is only a handful of places to fish.
