East Canyon Past Reports

Fishing is still okay. There is a lot of surface activity, but there not hitting good with spoons or spinners. The tube jig fishing is no longer good, so the only way to catch fish is trolling pop gear or dodgers with a worm. Most of the fish are around 6-12 inches with a few bigger ones here and there.
