East Canyon Past Reports

The lake is ice free, and fish are active on the surface. However, we where unable to catch any fish on spinners, spoons, tube jigs, or flies. The bite was on the bottom with half a worm and half a marshmallow. Fishing a bobber with half a worm about 3 feet below the bobber also worked. Most of the fish are 12 to 13 inches with a few larger here and there. (Report from: 4/19/19)
