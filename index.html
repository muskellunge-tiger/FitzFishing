Introduction

I am a fisherman from Utah with over ten years of fishing experience in Northern Utah. I have caught over a thousand fish, and have seen thousands of fish caught in those years. My favorite lake to fish Strawberry Reservoir, reasons being that it is the lake that I really learned how catch fish, and it is the place I have fished the longest, almost ten years, and the place I have caught the most fish out of. My favorite fish is the Tiger Muskie, some reasons for this is due to the fact that they are extremely hard to catch, and put up a fantastic fight once you hook them.

On the various pages on this site there will be: our current fishing reports, yearly fishing reports and strategies for the lake that we fished over a given year, and overall statistics for all the lakes and fish. All data on this site is from fall 2017 to the end of 2019. However, there will not be a specific part dedicated to 2017. New data will be added at the end of every year.

DISCLAIMER

All data and reports on this site are based off my own experiences. There for, if you want the offical Utah fishing report please use the link in the footer.
