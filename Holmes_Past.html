Holmes Past Reports

While there is a fair amount of fish in the lake that are in close and hitting the surface, the fishing is still a little slow. Spinners and spoons are quite doing good yet, and tube jigs are also not performing well either. The only success that we had was fishing with woolly bugger flies. We also some other people catch a few fish off the bottom using worms. But mainly for right now it seems as if the fishing is still slow. (Report from: 3/26/20)
