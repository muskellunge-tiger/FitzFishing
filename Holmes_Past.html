Holmes Past Reports

Holmes is almost ice free. Most of the pond is open water with plenty of fishing area. The water clarity is also fantastic. However, since it has been a while since it has been stocked there seems to be little to no fish in the pond. I would recommend waiting until it is stocked again before heading back out. (Report from: 3/20/19)
