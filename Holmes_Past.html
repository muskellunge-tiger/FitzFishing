Holmes Past Reports

After a recent stocking of another 1,700 fish the fishing is getting really good. Fishing with spoons is what we found to be the best option. Try using spoons like Kamloopers or Pot-O-Golds. The best colors are red, white, and gold. The fishing is best in the morning and early afternoon before it dies off in the middle of the day. (Report from: 4/13/19)

The fishing is getting fast after the recent stocking. Most fishing methods will catch fish. We found that fishing with a small red and white Kamlooper worked very well for fish on the surface. If you are fly fishing you will need to get your fly out a long way, or get in pontoon boat to be successful. We also saw other people catch stuff bottom fishing. All around the fishing is great. (Report from: 4/1/19)
